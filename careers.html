Recipient of multiple awards and arguably the most famous glass artist since Louis Comfort Tiffany, Dale Chihuly has been credited with elevating blown glass from delicate decorative object to groundbreaking fine art.

MOST RECENT AWARDS

  • 2007 Washington State Medal of Merit, Olympia, Washington Award of Appreciation, Art in Embassies Program, U.S. Department of State, Washington D.C.
  • 2011 Fritz Redlich Alumni Award, Institute of International Education, New York, New York Art Alliance for Contemporary Glass Honors Award, Chicago, Illinois
  • 2006 American Craft Council Gold Medal, Palm Beach, Florida Honorary Doctor of Humane Letters, University of Miami, Coral Gables, Florida
