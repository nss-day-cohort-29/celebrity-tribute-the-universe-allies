Executive Summary

Collaborators:

Although Chihuly has no direct projects in collaberation with another artists,
Chihuly often credits the crowd as his "team."
An article from The New York Times also states that Chihuly has his own personal team
that helps him prepare his exibits and his live art shows.

Known Aliases:

Sadly there are no entertaining names associated with Mr. Chihuly.
However, here are some words/phrases that could be used to describe him:
creator, designer, composer, virtuoso, a cool guy, a sick dude.

Country of residence:

You can find Chihuly living in the United States, more specifically, Seattle.
